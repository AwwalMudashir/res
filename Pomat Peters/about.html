POMAT PETERS NIGERIA LIMITED

is recognised as one of the world’s leading providers of advertising support needs to diverse businesses. As an industry leader, we have modern and technologically advanced large format digital and silk screen printing machines manned by an experienced and competent team.

On one hand, our screen printing department boasts of the most advanced and technologically improved machines in the entire Africa continent. On the other hand, our large format Digital Unit is equipped with highly sophisticated technological machines.

In addition, we are skillful in the production of bespoke outdoor and indoor advertising and promotional media that facilitate our clients’ marketing objectives. Our bespoke services give us a clear competitive advantage and endears us to the hearts of our major clients which include: Coca-Cola Nigeria Ltd., Guinness Nigeria Plc., PZ Industry Plc., American Embassy, Nigeria Distilleries Ltd, Nigerian Bottling Company Plc., Bates Cosse Ltd., LTC Advertising, to mention a few.

As we continue to seek innovative ways of improving the communication between our clients and the global world, we remain tenaciously committed to delivering first-class advertising support services to our numerous clients.

POMAT PETERS

started the business of marketing Screen-printing materials such as: inks, poster papers and allied chemicals under the name of Pomat Progressive Trading Company in 1978.

In 1979, another business outfit “Advertising Collection Agency” was added to the existing business. In that same year, the Company began a full-scale business of advertising contracting and screen process printing.

In 1986, the business was registered as POMAT PETERS NIGERIA COMPANY. At this time, the company was responsible for the production of stickers, handbills, banners, posters and other screen-printed materials. By the end of 1986, the Company acquired an acre of land in the Abule-Egba area of Lagos State; where the company’s factory is currently situated. The company was registered in 1989 under the Companies Act of 1968; as a Private Limited Liability Company – POMAT PETERS NIGERIA LIMITED.

Our resolution to support the changing needs of the advertising industry led to the acquisition of the most advanced technological equipments for Large Format Digital Printing in 2002. With the Large format digital systems; our production line is now fully automated with modern and sophisticated screen -printing machines and accessories. This places us at the cutting edge of producing high quality advertising materials whose quality is second to none.

POMAT PETERS NIGERIA LIMITED is currently staffed with diverse competent and intellectual talents who are highly skilled professionals in their select disciplines. Our production arm in particular is adequately staffed with highly-skilled and competent personnel whose skills and professional acumen are regularly updated through overseas and on-the-job training.
