What We Offer

Large Format Printing

We undertake large format printing on the following materials using a unique high-density inks injection, high resolution, ultra violet protected inks and high print speed, which offer sharp full color images and durability.

  • Self Adhesive Vinyl (SAV)
  • Frontlit flex
  • Backlit flex
  • Window graphics
  • Mesh

SIZES

We print all sizes of jobs ranging from conventional sizes like 4, 16, 40, 48, 96 sheets others include unipoles of different sizes like 36'X 16' , 48'X 16' , 50'X 15', 60'X 20' among many others. We also print un-conventional sizes to meet the request of the clients. Also we print and as well handle wall brandling.

Silk Screen Digital Printing

Our silk screen digital unit is the first of its kind in Sub-saharan African having the best assemblage of the latest equipments in the screen printing world.

We print on materials such as: Correx, ABS (A0, A1, A2, A3, A4 , A5 ,e.t.c) SAV, PVC producing classical support materials like Buntings , Danglers, Stickers, Pelmets, Banners, Table mat, Table talkers Lolly pop, Bar canopy, Fridge magnet, Bar canopy, Hangers , Wobblers, Fascias, Flag poles, Poly-ester banners, Floor mat just to mention a few.

Point of Sales Materials / Fabrication

We do not just print but also fabricate thereby providing our esteemed clients the best quality signage/point of sales materials that is suitable for them. These includes; Light box, Privilege panels , Kiosk, Display stand, Mobile trucks, Parasols, Canopies e.t.c
